# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and evidence-driven. Critique ideas, code, and process—not
people. Assume good intent while requiring reproducible claims.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, doxxing, and publication of private
information are not acceptable in issues, pull requests, reviews, or community
spaces associated with this project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer through GitHub. The
maintainer may edit or remove content, close discussions, or restrict participation
when needed to keep the project safe and constructive.
